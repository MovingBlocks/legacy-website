{% include status.html %} Terasology is a super duper extensible open source voxel-based Minecraft-like game. It was born from a Minecraft-inspired tech demo and is gradually becoming a stable platform for all sorts of gameplay settings in a voxel world.

From the ground up, Terasology was built to be a super hackable and modular game. Currently, Terasology has over 150 modules under the Terasology organisation, and many more which are maintained by individual enthusiasts. We welcome new ideas, both crazy and well thought-out for modules and game extensions from anyone and everyone, so feel free to drop by our IRC channel if you have any.

The creators and maintainers are a diverse mix of software developers, designers, game testers, graphic artists, musicians and even a few open source loving high schoolers. We encourage and appreciate contributions from everybody, and try to be as warm and welcoming as possible to newcomers.

Terasology is fully open source and is licensed under the Apache 2.0 license for code and under CC BY 4.0 for artwork (see credits for some minor exceptions).

If you have any queries or if you just want to hang out with us, do drop by our IRC channel on freenode, #terasology
