Terasology is a super extensible open source voxel-based game. Born from a Minecraft-inspired tech demo, it is gradually becoming a platform for all sorts of gameplay settings in a voxel world.

From the ground up, Terasology was built to be a modular and extensible game. We host a large number of modules on Github, and publish a playable bundle as "Omega" distribution for everyone to try out. We welcome new ideas, both crazy and well thought-out for modules and game extensions from anyone and everyone, so feel free to talk to us on our Discord.

The creators and maintainers are a diverse mix of software developers, designers, game testers, graphic artists, musicians and open source loving high schoolers. We encourage and appreciate contributions from everybody, and try to be as warm and welcoming as possible to newcomers.

Terasology is fully open source and is licensed under the Apache 2.0 license for code and under CC BY 4.0 for artwork.

We encourage contributions from anybody and try to keep a warm and friendly community and maintain a code of conduct. If you have any questions or if you just want to chat use this invite link for our Discord.
